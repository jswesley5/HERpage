2016

Artist Resurrection

  • Resigned With RCA in 2015
  • EP H.E.R Vol. 1 Released September 9th, 2016

H.e.r. emerged as an anonymous artist on soundcloud, she decided to keep her identity a secret until later that year.

2017

H.E.R. Offical 1st EP is Released

After releasing volume 2 of her first E.P. H.e.r. Hit the road with artist Bryson Tiller. Upon finishing the tour, she combined both volumes 1 and 2 of her self titled E.P. before headlining her first tour “Lights On”.

2018

Rising Star

2018 was a big year for this rising superstar. She began headlining shows across the world. Her song Best Part with Daniel Caesar took her straight to the top of the charts. Daniel Caesar even brought H.E.R. out to Coachella and his NPR Tiny Desk Concert. In the summer she traveled on tour with Chris Brown, during the tour she dropped Volume 1 of the I Used to Know HER EP a few months later, she released Volume 2 for the project. 2018 she was nominated for over 10 awards and won 2 and performed at multiple musical award shows including the B.E.T. awards and MTV Music Video Awards.

2019

H.E.R. 2nd E.P. is Released

H.E.R. began the year with winning her first two Grammy’s. At this point in her career, H.E.R. is really taking off. In 2019 she headlined her own stage at California music festival Coachella, and was invited to perform that summer at Chicago’s popular music festival Lollapalooza. At the end of the summer she released her second compilation album of I Used to Know HER. In September H.E.R. hosted her first Lights on Musical Festival, that she curated on her own. This festival included some of the biggest names in the musical industry like Jhene Aiko and Summer Walker.
